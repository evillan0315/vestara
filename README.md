# VESTARA

### Digital Investment & Funding Platform

## Project Proposal

### Executive Summary

Vestara is a modern digital investment platform designed to make investing accessible, transparent, and convenient for every Filipino. Through a secure web and mobile experience, users can deposit funds, participate in investment opportunities, track earnings, and withdraw profits using familiar payment channels such as GCash, Maya, and bank transfers.

Vestara lowers the barrier to entry by allowing investments starting at just ₱100 while enabling investors to participate in larger funding opportunities through a collective funding model.

The platform combines digital wallet functionality, investment product management, automated profit calculations, and comprehensive transaction tracking within a secure and scalable ecosystem.

---

# Vision

To become the leading digital investment marketplace in the Philippines by empowering individuals to grow their wealth through accessible, transparent, and technology-driven investment opportunities.

---

# Mission

Vestara's mission is to democratize investing by providing a secure platform where anyone can participate in funding opportunities regardless of investment size, while ensuring transparency, simplicity, and financial inclusion.

---

# Platform Overview

Vestara provides three primary services:

### Digital Wallet

Users can securely deposit, store, and manage funds within their Vestara account.

### Investment Marketplace

Users can browse and invest in active funding opportunities with defined funding goals, durations, and expected returns.

### Earnings & Withdrawals

Upon investment maturity, profits are automatically credited to the user's wallet and may be withdrawn through supported payment channels.

# Investment Workflow

1. User deposits funds.
2. Wallet balance is credited.
3. User selects an active investment product.
4. User chooses an investment amount.
5. Funds are allocated to the selected product.
6. Product becomes active when fully funded.
7. Investment matures after the specified duration.
8. Returns are automatically credited to the user's wallet.
9. User may reinvest or withdraw funds.
